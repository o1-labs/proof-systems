Deferred Computation

Let Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​ and Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​ be the two fields, with ∣Ep(Fq)∣=p|\mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q)| = p∣Ep​(Fq​)∣=p, ∣Eq(Fp)∣=q|\mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p)| = q∣Eq​(Fp​)∣=q for Elliptic curves Ep(Fq)\mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q)Ep​(Fq​) and Eq(Fp)\mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p)Eq​(Fp​). Assume q>pq > pq>p. We have a proof system (Kimchi) over Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​ and Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​, where commitments to public inputs are:

Pp=⟨e⃗,H⃗⟩∈Ep(Fq)Pq=⟨e⃗,G⃗⟩∈Eq(Fp)P_p = \langle \vec{e}, \vec{H} \rangle \in \mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q) \\ P_q = \langle \vec{e}, \vec{G} \rangle \in \mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p)Pp​=⟨e,H⟩∈Ep​(Fq​)Pq​=⟨e,G⟩∈Eq​(Fp​)

Respectively. See Pasta Curves for more details.

When referring to the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-side we mean the proof system for circuit over the field Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​.

Public Inputs / Why Passing​

In pickles-rs we have the notion of "passing" a variable (including the transcript) from one side to the other. e.g. when a field element α∈Fp\alpha \in \mathbb{F}_pα∈Fp​ needs to be used as a scalar on Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​.

This document explains what goes on "under the hood". Let us start by understanding why:

Let v∈Fqv \in \mathbb{F}_qv∈Fq​ be a scalar which we want to use to do both:

  1. Field arithmetic in Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​
  2. Scalar operations on Eq(Fp)\mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p)Eq​(Fp​)

In order to do so efficiently, we need to split these operations across two circuits (and therefore proofs) because:

  1. Emulating Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​ arithmetic in Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​ is very expensive, e.g. computing v⋅wv \cdot wv⋅w requires O(log⁡(q)2)O(\log(q)^2)O(log(q)2) multiplications over Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​: 100's of gates for a single multiplication.
  2. Since Eq(Fp)⊆Fp×Fp\mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p) \subseteq \mathbb{F}_p \times \mathbb{F}_pEq​(Fp​)⊆Fp​×Fp​ we cannot compute [v]⋅H∈Eq(Fp)[v] \cdot H \in \mathbb{E}_q(\mathbb{F}_p)[v]⋅H∈Eq​(Fp​) over Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​ efficiently, because, like before, emulating Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​ arithmetic in Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​ is very expensive...

Solution​

The solution is to "pass" a value vvv between the two proofs, in other words to have two values v~∈Fp\tilde{v} \in \mathbb{F}_pv~∈Fp​ and v∈Fqv \in \mathbb{F}_qv∈Fq​ which are equal as integers i.e. lift(v)=lift(v~)∈Z\text{lift}(v) = \text{lift}(\tilde{v}) \in \mathbb{Z}lift(v)=lift(v~)∈Z: they represent "the same number". A naive first attempt would be to simply add v~∈Fp\tilde{v} \in \mathbb{F}_pv~∈Fp​ to the witness on the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-side, however this has two problems:

Insufficient Field Size: p<qp < qp<q hence vvv cannot fit in Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​.

No Binding: More concerning, there is no binding between the v~\tilde{v}v~ in the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-witness and the vvv in the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-witness: a malicious prover could choose completely unrelated values. This violates soundness of the overall Fq/Fq\mathbb{F}_q/\mathbb{F}_qFq​/Fq​-relation being proved.

Problem 1: Decompose​

The solution to the first problem is simple:

In the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-side decompose v=2⋅h+lv = 2 \cdot h + lv=2⋅h+l with h∈[0,2⌊log⁡p⌋)h \in [0, 2^{\lfloor \log p \rfloor})h∈[0,2⌊logp⌋) (high bits) and l∈{0,1}l \in \{ 0, 1 \}l∈{0,1} (low bit). Note l,h<pl, h < pl,h<p since 2p>q2 p > q2p>q; always the case for any cycle of curves, ppp is only ≈q\approx \sqrt{q}≈q​ smaller than qqq, by Hasse. Now "v~\tilde{v}v~" is "represented" by the two values h~,l~∈Fp\tilde{h}, \tilde{l} \in \mathbb{F}_ph~,l~∈Fp​.

Note that no decomposition is necessary if the "original value" vvv was in Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​, since Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​ is big enough to hold the lift of any element in Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​.

Problem 2: Compute Commitment to the Public Input of other side​

To solve the binding issue we will add l,hl, hl,h to the public inputs on the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-side, for simplicity we will describe the case where l,hl, hl,h are the only public inputs in the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-side, which means that the commitment Pp∈Ep(Fq)P_p \in \mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q)Pp​∈Ep​(Fq​) to the public inputs on the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​ side is:

Pp=[h]⋅Gh+[l]⋅Gl∈Ep(Fq)P_p = [h] \cdot G_h + [l] \cdot G_l \in \mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q)Pp​=[h]⋅Gh​+[l]⋅Gl​∈Ep​(Fq​)

At this point it is important to note that Ep\mathbb{E}_pEp​ is defined over Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​!

Which means that we can compute Pp∈Ep(Fq)P_p \in \mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q)Pp​∈Ep​(Fq​) efficiently on the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-side!

Therefore to enforce the binding, we:

  1. Add a sub-circuit which checks: Pp=[h]⋅Gh+[l]⋅Gl∈Ep(Fq),∣h∣=⌊log⁡p⌋∣l∣=1P_p = [h] \cdot G_h + [l] \cdot G_l \in \mathbb{E}_p(\mathbb{F}_q), \\ |h| = \lfloor \log p \rfloor \\ |l| = 1Pp​=[h]⋅Gh​+[l]⋅Gl​∈Ep​(Fq​),∣h∣=⌊logp⌋∣l∣=1
  2. Add Pp=(x,y)∈Fq×FqP_p = (x, y) \in \mathbb{F}_q \times \mathbb{F}_qPp​=(x,y)∈Fq​×Fq​ to the public input on the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-side.

We recurse onwards...​

At this point the statement of the proof in Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-side is: the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-proof is sound, condition on providing an opening of PpP_pPp​ that satisfies the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-relation.

At this point you can stop and verify the proof (in the case of a "step proof" you would), by recomputing PpP_pPp​ outside the circuit while checking the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-relation manually "in the clear".

However, when recursing (e.g. wrapping in a "wrap proof") we need to "ingest" this public input PpP_pPp​; after all, to avoid blowup in the proof size everything (proofs/accumulators/public inputs etc.) must eventually become part of the witness and every computation covered by a circuit...

To this end, the wrap proof is a proof for the Fp\mathbb{F}_pFp​-relation with the public input PpP_pPp​ which additionally verifies the Fq\mathbb{F}_qFq​-proof.

The next "step" proof then verifies this wrap proof which means that PpP_pPp​ then becomes part of the witness!

In Pickles​

We can arbitrarily choose which side should compute the public input of the other, in pickles we let "wrap" compute the commitment to the public input.

Enforcing Equality​

Enforces that the public input of the proof verified on the Fr side is equal to the Fp input computed on Fr side.
