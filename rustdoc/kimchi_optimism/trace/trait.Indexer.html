impl Indexer for kimchi_optimism::keccak::column::ColumnAlias

The columns used by the Keccak circuit. The Keccak circuit is split into two main modes: Round and Sponge (split into Root, Absorb, Pad, RootPad, Squeeze). The columns are shared between the Sponge and Round steps (the total number of columns refers to the maximum of columns used by each mode) The hash, block, and step indices are shared between both modes.
